FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

Every demo ends with 15 minutes of Q&A with a solutions engineer.

Do the agents sound like AI? +

Not to most callers. We use neural voices with natural prosody, mid-sentence barge-in, and sub-600ms response times. You can keep the "this call may be monitored" disclosure or add explicit AI disclosure — it's a switch in the dashboard depending on your jurisdiction.

How do we stay compliant with TCPA and HIPAA? +

We maintain SOC 2 Type II and sign BAAs for healthcare deployments. Consent, opt-outs, and quiet-hours are enforced at the platform layer. For outbound SMS and voice, we validate consent source against your CRM before every send.

Can we keep our existing phone numbers? +

Yes. We SIP-forward from your carrier — Twilio, RingCentral, Dialpad, Vonage, or your existing PBX. Your DID, your CNAM, your voicemail fallback. Nothing changes for callers.

What happens when the agent doesn't know the answer? +

Graceful handoff. The call warm-transfers to the right human with a one-line context summary, transcript, and caller intent. For SMS, we escalate to a defined Slack/Teams channel or a team inbox. Escalation thresholds are tuned weekly.

Is this a platform, a tool, or a service? +

All three. You get the platform (dashboards, APIs, real-time transcripts), the agents (voice + SMS, trained on your data), and the service layer — a named solutions engineer and success manager who own the outcome. Most customers hire us for the outcome.

How is this priced? +

Monthly platform fee plus usage (per-minute voice, per-message SMS). We scope to your volume on the first call and give you a fixed-cap estimate for the first 90 days so you can forecast. No seat licenses, no per-agent nonsense.
